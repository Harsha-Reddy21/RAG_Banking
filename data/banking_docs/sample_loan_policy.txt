1. INTRODUCTION
1.1 Purpose
This Loan Policy Manual establishes guidelines for the Bank of Example's lending activities. It is designed to ensure that all lending activities are conducted in a safe, sound manner and in compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

1.2 Scope
This policy applies to all loan officers, credit analysts, and other personnel involved in the lending function at Bank of Example.

3. INTEREST RATES
3.1 Rate Setting
Interest rates are set based on the following factors:
- Current market conditions
- Federal Reserve benchmark rates
- Loan type and term
- Borrower creditworthiness
- Loan-to-value ratio

Table 3.1: Base Interest Rate Ranges (as of January 2023)
| Loan Type | Excellent Credit (740+) | Good Credit (700-739) | Fair Credit (660-699) |
|-----------|------------------------|----------------------|----------------------|
| Personal Loan | 7.50% - 9.99% | 10.00% - 12.99% | 13.00% - 17.99% |
| Mortgage (30yr Fixed) | 5.75% - 6.25% | 6.26% - 6.75% | 6.76% - 7.50% |
| Commercial | 6.50% - 8.50% | 8.51% - 10.50% | 10.51% - 13.00% |

3.2 Rate Adjustments
Adjustable rate mortgages (ARMs) are subject to rate adjustments based on the terms specified in the loan agreement. Rate adjustments are tied to the 1-year Treasury Constant Maturity rate plus a margin of 2.75%.

Table 3.2: ARM Adjustment Caps
| ARM Type | Initial Adjustment Cap | Subsequent Adjustment Cap | Lifetime Cap |
|----------|------------------------|---------------------------|--------------|
| 5/1 ARM | 2% | 2% | 5% |
| 7/1 ARM | 2% | 2% | 5% |
| 10/1 ARM | 2% | 2% | 5% |

6. REGULATORY COMPLIANCE
6.1 Equal Credit Opportunity Act (ECOA)
The Bank of Example complies with all provisions of the Equal Credit Opportunity Act. The Bank does not discriminate against any applicant on the basis of race, color, religion, national origin, sex, marital status, age, receipt of public assistance, or good faith exercise of rights under the Consumer Credit Protection Act.

6.2 Fair Housing Act
For mortgage loans, the Bank complies with all provisions of the Fair Housing Act and does not discriminate in the sale, rental, or financing of housing.

6.3 Truth in Lending Act (TILA)
The Bank provides all disclosures required by the Truth in Lending Act, including the Annual Percentage Rate (APR), finance charge, amount financed, total of payments, and payment schedule.

6.4 Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act (RESPA)
For mortgage loans, the Bank complies with all provisions of RESPA, including providing the Good Faith Estimate, Servicing Disclosure Statement, and HUD-1 Settlement Statement.

6.5 Community Reinvestment Act (CRA)
The Bank is committed to meeting the credit needs of the communities in which it operates, including low- and moderate-income neighborhoods, consistent with safe and sound banking operations.
